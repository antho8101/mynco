About Mynco

Mynco is the ultimate client portal platform for freelancers. We transform how freelancers deliver projects, impress clients, and get paid faster with beautiful, branded client experiences.

Founded by Anthony Carayon, Mynco was born from a personal need for better client management tools. When existing solutions were either too complicated, too expensive, or missing essential features, Anthony decided to build the perfect tool himself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from journalists

What makes Mynco different from other client portal solutions?

Mynco is specifically designed for freelancers, with a focus on simplicity, speed, and professional presentation. Unlike enterprise solutions, we prioritize ease of use and quick setup while maintaining enterprise-level quality.

How does Mynco help freelancers get paid faster?

By providing a professional, branded experience that builds client trust and confidence. Our streamlined payment integration and clear project delivery process reduces payment delays and disputes.

What's your business model?

We operate on a subscription model with transparent pricing at €29/month. No hidden fees, no per-client charges, and no setup costs.

What are your future plans?

We're focused on expanding our feature set based on user feedback, improving our mobile experience, and growing our presence in European markets.

How do you ensure data security?

We use industry-standard encryption, secure hosting on Vercel, and follow GDPR compliance. Client data is protected with enterprise-grade security measures.

What's the story behind the name "Mynco"?

Mynco represents "My Company" - the idea that every freelancer should have the tools to present themselves as a professional company, regardless of their size.
