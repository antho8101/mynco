The story behind Mynco

Created by an entrepreneur who needed the perfect tool for client portals. When none fit the bill, he decided to build it himself.

A personal problem, a universal solution

As a freelancer and entrepreneur, I constantly needed tools to manage my client projects. But none really suited me: either too complicated, too expensive, or missing essential features.

Instead of continuing to use imperfect solutions, I decided to create the tool that would suit me perfectly. That's how Mynco was born.

The idea was simple: create an intuitive, affordable, and complete platform that meets the real needs of freelancers and their clients.
